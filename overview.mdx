> **TL;DR:** Boundless is a Web3 collaboration platform where every payment is protected by trustless escrow and every contribution builds your on-chain reputation.

## Welcome

Whether you're a designer hunting for bounties, a researcher applying for grants, a developer joining a hackathon, or a creator launching a crowdfunding campaign this is your guide to Boundless.

**No blockchain experience? No problem.** Boundless is designed to feel like a normal web app: sign up with email, the platform manages your balance (no wallet or seed phrase), and payments use escrow powered by [Trustless Work](https://www.trustlesswork.com/).

<Note title="What's live today">
  **Hackathons** and **crowdfunding** (Projects) are available now. **Bounties**, **grants**, and **off-ramping** (XLM -> fiat) are upcoming. Withdrawing your Stellar assets from the platform requires [KYC](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification) (powered by [Didit](https://didit.me/)).
</Note>

## Core concepts

Before diving into a specific module, understand the foundation:

| Concept                                                  | Why it matters                                                                            |
| -------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [Trustless escrow](/concepts/trustless-escrow-explained) | Every payment is locked until work is verified. No trust required.                        |
| [Reputation system](/concepts/reputation-system)         | Your on-chain track record unlocks bigger opportunities across all modules.               |
| [Wallets and accounts](/concepts/passkey-wallets)        | Boundless manages your balance no seed phrase or wallet app. Passkey sign-in coming soon. |
| [Power Cells](/concepts/power-cells)                     | Application credits help prevent spam. Earn them by completing work.                      |

## Quick start paths

**I want to earn money:**

1. [Set up your account](/getting-started/quick-start) (about 5 minutes)
2. [Join a hackathon](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz/hackathons) (bounties coming soon)
3. [Complete KYC](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification) when you need to withdraw Stellar assets

**I want to fund a project:**

1. [Create an account](/getting-started/quick-start)
2. [Launch a campaign](/modules/crowdfunding/creating-campaigns) (grants coming soon)
3. Define your milestones; escrow is powered by [Trustless Work](https://www.trustlesswork.com/)

**I want to hire or run events:**

1. [Set up your project account](/getting-started/quick-start)
2. [Organize a hackathon](/modules/hackathons/organizing) or [create bounties](/modules/bounties/creating-bounties) when bounties launch
