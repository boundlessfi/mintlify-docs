> **TL;DR:** Boundless is a Web3 collaboration platform where every payment is protected by trustless escrow and every contribution builds your on-chain reputation.

## Welcome

Whether you're a designer hunting for bounties, a researcher applying for grants, a developer joining a hackathon, or a creator launching a crowdfunding campaign this is your guide to Boundless.

**No blockchain experience? No problem.** Boundless is designed to feel as simple as any web app: **passkey login(coming soon)**, instant payments, automatic escrow.

## Core concepts

Before diving into a specific module, understand the foundation:

| Concept                                                  | Why it matters                                                                       |
| -------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| [Trustless escrow](/concepts/trustless-escrow-explained) | Every payment is locked until work is verified. No trust required.                   |
| [Reputation system](/concepts/reputation-system)         | Your on-chain track record unlocks bigger opportunities across all modules.          |
| [Passkey wallets](/concepts/passkey-wallets)             | Sign up with your fingerprint or security key. No seed phrases, no wallet downloads. |
| [Power Cells](/concepts/power-cells)                     | Application credits help prevent spam. Earn them by completing work.                 |

## Quick start paths

**I want to earn money:**

1. [Set up your account](/getting-started/quick-start) (about 5 minutes)
2. [Browse bounties](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz/bounties) or [join a hackathon](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz/hackathons)
3. [Complete KYC](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification) to unlock higher-value work and off-ramping

**I want to fund a project:**

1. [Create an account](/getting-started/quick-start)
2. [Apply for a grant](/modules/grants/application-process) or [launch a campaign](/modules/crowdfunding/creating-campaigns)
3. Define your milestones and escrow your budget

**I want to hire contributors:**

1. [Set up your project account](/getting-started/quick-start)
2. [Post a bounty](/modules/bounties/creating-bounties) or [organize a hackathon](/modules/hackathons/organizing)
3. Review submissions and release payments on delivery
