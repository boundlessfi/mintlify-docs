This guide walks you through converting XLM (or other supported assets) to fiat using Boundless [off-ramping](/concepts/off-ramping).

## Prerequisites

- XLM in your Boundless wallet (e.g., from bounties, grants, or rewards).
- [KYC verification](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification) completed if required for your region.
- You are in a [supported country](/reference/supported-countries) for off-ramping.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open off-ramp">
    In the Boundless app, go to the off-ramp or withdraw section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select amount and destination">
    Choose how much XLM to convert and where to send fiat (e.g., bank account, card). Review the exchange rate and fees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm the transaction. The provider will process the conversion and send fiat according to their timeline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fees and limits

Fees and limits depend on the off-ramp provider and your verification level. See [Fee structure](/reference/fee-structure) for platform fees; provider terms apply for conversion and transfer.
