> **TL;DR:** Off-ramping (converting XLM to fiat and sending to your bank or card) is an **upcoming feature** on Boundless. **Withdrawing** your Stellar assets from the platform **requires [KYC](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification)** (powered by [Didit](https://didit.me/)). This page describes the flow for when off-ramp is available.

## Status: coming soon

Off-ramping is not yet available. When it launches, you will need to complete KYC before you can convert XLM to fiat and withdraw. The steps below are for reference.

## Prerequisites (when available)

- XLM in your Boundless balance (e.g., from hackathons, crowdfunding, or future bounties/grants).
- [KYC verification](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification) completed required to withdraw Stellar assets.
- You are in a [supported country](/reference/supported-countries) for off-ramping.

## Steps (when available)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open off-ramp">
    In the Boundless app, go to the off-ramp or withdraw section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select amount and destination">
    Choose how much XLM to convert and where to send fiat (e.g., bank account, card). Review the exchange rate and fees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm the transaction. The provider will process the conversion and send fiat according to their timeline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fees and limits

Fees and limits will depend on the off-ramp provider and your verification level. See [Fee structure](/reference/fee-structure) for platform fees; provider terms apply for conversion and transfer.
