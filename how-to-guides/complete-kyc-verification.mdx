---
title: "Complete KYC verification"
description: "How to complete identity verification (KYC) on Boundless."
---

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification may be required to use certain features on Boundless, such as [off-ramping](/concepts/off-ramping) XLM to fiat. This guide outlines the process.

## When KYC is required

- **Off-ramping** Converting XLM to fiat typically requires verified identity.
- **Some programs** Certain grant programs or campaigns may require KYC for compliance.

Check the feature or program for exact requirements.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start verification">
    In the Boundless app, open the verification or account settings section and start the KYC flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide identity documents">
    Submit the requested ID (e.g., passport, national ID) and any selfie or liveness check as prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for review">
    The provider reviews your submission. You’ll be notified when verification is complete or if more information is needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported countries

KYC and off-ramping availability depend on jurisdiction. See [Supported countries](/reference/supported-countries).
