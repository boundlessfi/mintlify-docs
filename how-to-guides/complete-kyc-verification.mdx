> **TL;DR:** Boundless uses [Didit](https://didit.me/) for identity verification (KYC). You need to complete KYC before you can **withdraw** your Stellar assets (e.g., to an external wallet or to fiat). This guide outlines the process.

## What you'll learn

- When KYC is required (withdrawals)
- That verification is powered by Didit
- The steps to complete verification
- Where to check supported countries

---

## When KYC is required

**Withdrawing your Stellar assets** (e.g., XLM) from Boundless requires KYC. That includes:

- Withdrawing to an external wallet
- Converting to fiat (off-ramping) when that feature is available

So: you can earn and hold a balance without KYC, but to **take assets off the platform** you must complete identity verification.

Some programs (e.g., certain grant or campaign rules) may also require KYC for compliance; check the program details.

---

## How Boundless does KYC: Didit

Boundless partners with [Didit](https://didit.me/) for KYC. Didit is an identity platform that handles ID verification, liveness checks, and related compliance. Your verification runs through their flow; Boundless receives the result (e.g., approved, declined, or in review).

---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start verification">
    In the Boundless app, open the verification or account settings section and start the KYC flow. You'll be guided to Didit's verification experience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the checks">
    Follow the prompts: you may be asked for an ID (e.g., passport or national ID), a selfie, and/or a liveness check. Didit runs these in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the result">
    Didit reviews your submission. You'll be notified when verification is complete or if more information is needed. Boundless will then know whether you're verified for withdrawals.
  </Step>
</Steps>
