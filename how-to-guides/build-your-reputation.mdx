---
title: "Build your reputation"
description: "Practical steps to build reputation on Boundless."
---

[Reputation](/concepts/reputation-system) on Boundless reflects your track record. Higher reputation can improve your chances of being selected for bounties, grants, and hackathons, and can help backers and funders trust you.

## What helps

- **Complete bounties** Deliver quality work and close bounties successfully.
- **Deliver grant milestones** Submit on time and meet the stated criteria so milestones are approved.
- **Participate in hackathons** Submit valid projects and complete post-event milestones when applicable.
- **Deliver on campaigns** If you run a crowdfunding campaign, deliver on your [milestone structure](/modules/crowdfunding/milestone-structure).
- **Avoid disputes** Resolve issues constructively; see [Handle disputes](/how-to-guides/handle-disputes).

## What hurts

- Uncompleted work, rejected milestones, or failed deliveries.
- Disputes that resolve against you or repeated policy violations.

## Tips

- Start with smaller bounties or clear grant milestones to build a positive history.
- Communicate clearly with funders or backers if you need more time or scope changes.
