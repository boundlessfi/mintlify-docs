Connect with other users, builders, and the Boundless team through our community channels.

## Where to find us

- **Discord:** Join the Boundless Discord server (link in the [Boundless app](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz) or website) for chat, support, and announcements.
- **Telegram:** Join the Boundless Telegram group (link in the app or website) for discussions and updates.

Links to these channels are available in the [Boundless app](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz) footer or [Contact us](/support/contact-us) page.

## What to expect

- Product updates and announcements
- Peer support and discussions about bounties, grants, hackathons, and crowdfunding
- Feedback and feature discussions

Please follow each channel’s rules and be respectful of other members. For official support or security issues, use [Contact us](/support/contact-us) or [Report a bug](/support/report-a-bug).
