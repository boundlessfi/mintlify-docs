Crowdfunding on [Boundless](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz) lets creators raise funds and backers support projects with community validation and milestone-based releases.

## As a creator

1. [Set up your account](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet).
2. Read [creating campaigns](/modules/crowdfunding/creating-campaigns) and [community validation](/modules/crowdfunding/community-validation).
3. Define your [milestone structure](/modules/crowdfunding/milestone-structure) and launch on [Boundless](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz/projects).

## As a backer

1. [Set up your account](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet).
2. [Browse campaigns](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz/projects) and read [backing campaigns](/modules/crowdfunding/backing-campaigns).
3. Back a campaign and track milestone delivery.
