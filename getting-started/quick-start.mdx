## Before you start

- No blockchain experience required Boundless is built to feel like a normal web app

---

## Step 1: Create your account

Go to [Boundless](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz) and sign up with your email and the sign-in method the app offers (e.g., password). You don't need a wallet or seed phrase, Boundless manages your balance for you.

<Check title="Done when">
  You can log in and see your profile or dashboard.
</Check>

---

## Step 2: Your account and balance

Boundless **abstracts wallets**: the platform holds your balance (XLM and other supported assets) so you don't need a separate wallet app or seed phrase. Payouts from bounties, grants, hackathons, or crowdfunding go to your Boundless balance. You can add funds or withdraw when the app supports it.

For more detail, see [Set up your account and balance](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet).

---

## Step 3: Complete KYC (if you need it)

KYC (identity verification) is required for:

- [Off-ramping](/concepts/off-ramping) (converting XLM to fiat)
- Some grant programs or higher-value features

If you only want to earn XLM and use it on the platform, you can skip KYC for now. When you're ready, see [Complete KYC verification](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification).
