[Hackathons](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz/hackathons) on Boundless let you build, compete, and ship with clear judging and post-event milestones.

## As a participant

1. [Set up your account](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet) and optionally [form a team](/how-to-guides/form-a-hackathon-team).
2. Read [participating](/modules/hackathons/participating) and [browse hackathons](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz/hackathons) to find events and submit projects.
3. Understand the [judging system](/modules/hackathons/judging-system) and [post-event milestones](/modules/hackathons/post-event-milestones).

## As an organizer

1. Review [organizing](/modules/hackathons/organizing) for event setup and rules.
2. Configure the [judging system](/modules/hackathons/judging-system) and [post-event milestones](/modules/hackathons/post-event-milestones) for your hackathon.
