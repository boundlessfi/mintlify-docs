Grants fund projects through applications and milestone-based payouts. This guide walks you through applying for your first grant.

## Before you start

- [Set up your wallet](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet)
- Optional: [Build your reputation](/how-to-guides/build-your-reputation)

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a grant program">
    Browse [grant programs](/modules/grants/grant-programs) and choose one that fits your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understand grant types">
    Read [grant types](/modules/grants/grant-types) and the [application process](/modules/grants/application-process).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your application">
    Complete the application with your project description, milestones, and any required details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deliver milestones">
    Once accepted, follow [milestone delivery](/modules/grants/milestone-delivery) to receive payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>
