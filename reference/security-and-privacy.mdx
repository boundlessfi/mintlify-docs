Boundless is designed to keep your assets and data secure while giving you control over your wallet and identity.

## Account and balance

- **Managed wallets** Boundless [abstracts wallets](/concepts/passkey-wallets) for you: the platform holds your balance. You sign in with email (and the method the app provides). **Passkey sign-in is planned for the future.** Never share your login credentials; anyone with access could use your account.
- **Escrow** Funds are held in [trustless escrow](/concepts/trustless-escrow-explained); release follows programmatic rules, not a single custodian.

## Data and privacy

- **Identity** KYC data is used for verification and compliance and is handled according to our privacy policy and applicable law.
- **Usage data** We collect and use data necessary to operate the platform, prevent abuse, and improve the product. See our privacy policy for details.

## Best practices

- Use a strong, unique password for your Boundless account.
- Do not share your login credentials (email/password or recovery details) with anyone.
- Verify URLs and only use the official Boundless app or site.

## Reporting issues

If you find a security vulnerability, see [Report a bug](/support/report-a-bug).
