---
title: "Glossary"
description: "Definitions of key terms used in Boundless documentation."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Backer">
    A user who contributes funds to a crowdfunding campaign.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bounty">
    A task with a reward, held in escrow until a claimer completes the work and is selected.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Claiming model">
    The rule set for how a bounty reward is assigned (e.g., first to complete, best submission).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Campaign">
    A crowdfunding initiative with a goal and optional milestones.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Didit">
    The identity platform Boundless uses for KYC (identity verification). See [didit.me](https://didit.me/). Withdrawing Stellar assets from Boundless requires completing KYC via Didit.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Escrow">
    Funds locked until conditions are met. Boundless uses [Trustless Work](https://www.trustlesswork.com/) for escrow infrastructure on Stellar: trustless, non-custodial, milestone-based.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Grant">
    Application-based funding with optional milestone-based payouts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Grant program">
    A funding initiative with defined scope, eligibility, and rules.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lightning round">
    A time-limited window for submitting claims to a bounty.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Milestone">
    A defined deliverable that, when approved, triggers a release of funds (grants or crowdfunding).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Off-ramping">
    Converting crypto (e.g., XLM) to fiat currency. **Upcoming feature** on Boundless. Withdrawing Stellar assets from the platform already requires KYC.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Trustless Work">
    The escrow infrastructure Boundless uses for secure, non-custodial, milestone-based payments on Stellar. See [trustlesswork.com](https://www.trustlesswork.com/).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Managed wallet / abstracted wallet">
    Boundless holds your balance for you; you don't set up a seed phrase or external wallet. Your account has a balance the platform manages. Passkey sign-in is planned for the future.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Passkey (coming soon)">
    A future way to sign in or approve actions using biometrics or a security key instead of (or in addition to) a password.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Spark">
    The platform credit used only for bounties (e.g., spending to apply, earning by completing work).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reputation">
    A score or history reflecting your outcomes and behavior on the platform.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Trustless">
    Not relying on a single trusted party; rules are enforced by the protocol or system.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="XLM">
    Stellar Lumens, the native asset of the Stellar network used for payments and fees on Boundless.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
