Boundless availability including [KYC](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification) and [off-ramping](/concepts/off-ramping) depends on your country or region. Restrictions are in place for regulatory and provider reasons.

## What varies by region

- **Platform access** General use of bounties, grants, hackathons, and crowdfunding may be limited in some jurisdictions.
- **KYC and off-ramping** Identity verification and XLM-to-fiat conversion are only offered where our providers support them.

## How to check

- When you sign up or use a feature (e.g., off-ramp), the app will show whether your region is supported or if the feature is unavailable.
- For the latest list of supported countries, check the Boundless app or contact [Support](/support/contact-us).

## Your responsibility

You are responsible for complying with local laws. Do not use VPNs or false information to access the platform from an unsupported or restricted region.
