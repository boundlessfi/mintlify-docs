Boundless availability, including [KYC](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification) (powered by [Didit](https://didit.me/)) and [off-ramping](/concepts/off-ramping) when it launches, depends on your country or region. **Withdrawing** your Stellar assets from the platform requires KYC. Restrictions are in place for regulatory and provider reasons.

## What varies by region

- **Platform access:** Use of hackathons, crowdfunding (Projects), and (when available) bounties and grants may be limited in some jurisdictions.
- **KYC and withdrawals:** Identity verification is required to withdraw Stellar assets. KYC is provided by Didit; availability depends on what Didit supports in your region.
- **Off-ramping:** Converting XLM to fiat is an upcoming feature; when available, it will depend on region and provider support.

## How to check

- When you sign up or use a feature (e.g., off-ramp), the app will show whether your region is supported or if the feature is unavailable.
- For the latest list of supported countries, check the [Boundless app](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz) or contact [Support](/support/contact-us).

## Your responsibility

You are responsible for complying with local laws. Do not use VPNs or false information to access the platform from an unsupported or restricted region.
