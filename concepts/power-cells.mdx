> **TL;DR:** Power Cells are credits you spend on actions like applying to bounties. You earn them by completing work and through rewards. Running out blocks some actions until you earn more.

## What you'll learn

- What Power Cells are and what they're used for
- When you spend them (e.g., applying to a bounty)
- How to earn more
- Where to see your balance and costs

---

## What Power Cells are for

Power Cells help prevent spam and low-quality applications. You spend them when you:

- Apply to bounties (often 1 Power Cell per application)
- Create bounties, campaigns, or events (depending on product rules)
- Use other premium or rate-limited actions

The exact cost is shown before you confirm (e.g., "This application will use 1 Power Cell").

---

## Earning Power Cells

You can earn Power Cells by:

- Completing bounties and getting approved
- Delivering grant milestones
- Participating in hackathons (e.g., submitting, winning, or completing post-event milestones)
- Other rewards or promotions (see in-app or announcements)

<Warning title="Important">
  If an action costs Power Cells, make sure you have enough before you submit. Failed or rejected applications may or may not refund the Cell depending on the rules for that bounty.
</Warning>

---

## Spending Power Cells

- **Applying to bounties** — Many bounties require 1 Power Cell per application. You spend it when you click submit; if you're not selected, the Cell may be returned when the bounty is resolved (check the bounty rules).
- **Creating bounties or campaigns** — Creation may cost Power Cells; the app will show the cost before you confirm.

Always check the confirmation screen for the exact cost.
