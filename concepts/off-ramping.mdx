## What is off-ramping?

When you earn XLM on Boundless (from bounties, grants, hackathons, or crowdfunding), you can:

- **Keep it** in your wallet and use it on the platform.
- **Send it** to another wallet.
- **Convert it to fiat** (e.g., USD, EUR, NGN) and withdraw to your bank or card that's **off-ramping**.

Off-ramping is useful when you want to spend or save in your local currency instead of holding crypto.

---

## Before you start

- **XLM in your wallet** You need a balance to convert.
- **KYC completed** Identity verification is usually required. See [Complete KYC verification](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification).
- **Supported country** Off-ramping isn't available everywhere. See [Supported countries](/reference/supported-countries).

---

## How it works

1. In the Boundless app, open the off-ramp or withdraw section.
2. Choose how much XLM to convert and where to send the fiat (e.g., bank account, card).
3. Review the rate and any fees.
4. Confirm. The provider converts XLM to fiat and sends the funds according to their timeline.

Fees and limits depend on the provider and your verification level. See [Fee structure](/reference/fee-structure) for platform fees; the off-ramp provider's terms apply for conversion and transfer.
