## Status: coming soon

**Off-ramping** (converting XLM or other Stellar assets to fiat and withdrawing to your bank or card) is planned for a future release. This page describes how it will work and what you need to know now.

**Today:** If you want to **withdraw** your Stellar assets from Boundless (e.g., to an external wallet), you must complete [KYC verification](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification). KYC is powered by [Didit](https://didit.me/).

---

## When off-ramping is available

You will typically need:

- **Balance** XLM (or supported assets) in your Boundless balance to convert.
- **KYC completed** Identity verification is required to withdraw/off-ramp. See [Complete KYC verification](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification).
- **Supported country** Availability will depend on region. See [Supported countries](/reference/supported-countries).

---

## How it will work

1. In the Boundless app, open the off-ramp or withdraw section.
2. Choose how much to convert and where to send the fiat (e.g., bank account, card).
3. Review the rate and any fees.
4. Confirm. The provider converts assets to fiat and sends funds according to their timeline.

Fees and limits will depend on the provider and your verification level.
