Bounties on Boundless are tasks with a reward. Funders lock payment in [trustless escrow](/concepts/trustless-escrow-explained); claimers complete the work and receive the reward when conditions are met.

## Key concepts

- **Bounty** A task plus a reward (in XLM or other assets), held in escrow until completion.
- **Claiming model** How the reward is assigned (e.g., first-come, best submission, judge choice). See [Four claiming models](/modules/bounties/four-claiming-models).
- **Lightning round** A time-limited window for claiming. See [Lightning rounds](/modules/bounties/lightning-rounds).

## For claimers

- Browse open bounties and filter by skill, reward, or claiming model.
- [Apply for bounties](/modules/bounties/applying-for-bounties) according to the rules of each bounty.
- Build [reputation](/concepts/reputation-system) by delivering quality work.

## For creators

- [Create bounties](/modules/bounties/creating-bounties) by defining the task, reward, and claiming model.
- Fund escrow; once the task is completed and verified, funds release to the winner(s).
