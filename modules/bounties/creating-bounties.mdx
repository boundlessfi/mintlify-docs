---
title: "Creating bounties"
description: "How to create and fund a bounty on Boundless."
---

You create a bounty by defining the task, reward, and claiming model, then locking the reward in [trustless escrow](/concepts/trustless-escrow-explained).

## Before you create

- [Set up your wallet](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet) with enough XLM (and [Spark](/concepts/power-cells) if required).
- Have a clear task description and acceptance criteria.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define the bounty">
    Write the task title, description, deliverables, and acceptance criteria. Be specific so claimers know what to do.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose claiming model">
    Select one of the [four claiming models](/modules/bounties/four-claiming-models): first to complete, best submission, split, or custom.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the reward">
    Set the reward amount (XLM or other supported asset). This amount will be locked in escrow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund escrow">
    Lock the reward in escrow. The bounty becomes open for claims once funded.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and publish">
    Optionally set a deadline or [lightning round](/modules/bounties/lightning-rounds). Publish the bounty.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After publishing

- Claimers will submit according to the claiming model.
- You (or a judge) review and select the winner(s); funds release from escrow to them.
- If there’s a dispute, see [Handle disputes](/how-to-guides/handle-disputes).
