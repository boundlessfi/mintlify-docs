---

To claim a bounty, you submit work (or a claim) according to the bounty’s rules and claiming model. This guide covers how to find bounties and apply.

## Before you apply

- [Set up your wallet](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet).
- Optional: [Build your reputation](/how-to-guides/build-your-reputation).
- Read the bounty description, deadline, and [claiming model](/modules/bounties/four-claiming-models).

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a bounty">
    Browse the Bounties section. Filter by skill, reward size, or claiming model.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the rules">
    Check requirements, deliverables, and how the winner is chosen (first to complete, best submission, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your claim">
    Submit your work or application as specified (link, file, form). For lightning rounds, submit before the round closes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for resolution">
    The creator or judge reviews submissions and selects the winner(s). Funds release from escrow to the winner(s).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips

- Deliver exactly what the bounty asks for to avoid disputes.
- For best-submission bounties, present your work clearly so judges can evaluate it fairly.
