> **TL;DR:** Crowdfunding on Boundless lets creators raise funds and backers support them. Money is held in escrow and released as milestones are approved. In the app you'll see this under **Projects** ([boundlessfi.xyz/projects](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz/projects)).

## What you'll learn

- What a campaign is and how escrow protects backers
- How milestones and community validation work
- How to create a campaign or back one
- Where to find campaigns in the app

---

## What is crowdfunding on Boundless?

Creators launch **campaigns** with a funding goal and (optionally) a **milestone structure**. Backers contribute XLM; funds go into **escrow**. When the creator completes a milestone and it's approved, that portion of the funds is **released** to the creator. If milestones aren't met, backers are protected by the escrow rules.

So: backers don't pay upfront into a black box; creators get paid incrementally as they deliver.

---

## Key concepts

| Concept | What it means |
|--------|----------------|
| **Campaign** | A fundraising goal, description, and (optionally) milestones. In the app: a **Project**. |
| **Milestone structure** | How much of the raised funds release for each deliverable. See [Milestone structure](/modules/crowdfunding/milestone-structure). |
| **Community validation** | When the community (e.g., backers) can vote or signal on milestone releases. See [Community validation](/modules/crowdfunding/community-validation). |
| **Backing** | Contributing XLM to a campaign. See [Backing campaigns](/modules/crowdfunding/backing-campaigns). |

## Where to find it in the app

- **Browse campaigns:** [Projects](https://www.boundlessfi.xyz/projects)
- **Your campaigns:** **Me** -> **Projects** / **Crowdfunding**
- **Create a campaign:** **Create project** / **Launch campaign** (from Projects or your dashboard)

Share links for campaigns use the form: `boundlessfi.xyz/campaigns/{projectId}`.

## For creators

1. [Create a campaign](/modules/crowdfunding/creating-campaigns): set your goal, description, and milestones.
2. Optional: use [community validation](/modules/crowdfunding/community-validation) if the product supports it.
3. Deliver on your [milestone structure](/modules/crowdfunding/milestone-structure) so funds release from escrow to you.

---

## For backers

1. [Back campaigns](/modules/crowdfunding/backing-campaigns) with XLM.
2. Track progress and milestone releases in the app.
3. Optionally participate in community validation if the campaign uses it.
