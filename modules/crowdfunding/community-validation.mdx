---
title: "Community validation"
description: "How community validation works for crowdfunding campaigns on Boundless."
---

Community validation lets the community (e.g., backers or designated validators) influence or approve steps in a campaign such as unlocking funds for a milestone or verifying that a goal was met.

## How it works

- **Validation triggers** The campaign creator defines when validation is needed (e.g., before releasing a milestone, or to approve a change).
- **Who validates** Depending on setup: backers vote, designated validators approve, or the community signals in a defined way.
- **Outcome** If validation passes, the corresponding action (e.g., release of funds) proceeds; otherwise it may be delayed or reverted according to campaign rules.

## For creators

- When [creating campaigns](/modules/crowdfunding/creating-campaigns), you can enable community validation and set the rules (who votes, threshold, etc.).
- Use it to build trust: backers see that releases are gated on community approval.

## For backers

- You may be asked to vote or signal on milestone releases or other campaign decisions. Your participation helps keep campaigns accountable.
