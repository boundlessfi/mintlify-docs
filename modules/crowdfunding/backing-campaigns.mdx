---
title: "Backing campaigns"
description: "How to back a crowdfunding campaign on Boundless."
---

Backing a campaign means contributing XLM (or other supported assets) to a creator’s funding goal. Your funds are held in [trustless escrow](/concepts/trustless-escrow-explained) and released according to the campaign’s [milestone structure](/modules/crowdfunding/milestone-structure) when the creator delivers and (if applicable) [community validation](/modules/crowdfunding/community-validation) passes.

## Before you back

- [Set up your wallet](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet) with XLM to contribute.
- Read the campaign description, goal, and milestone structure so you know how and when funds are released.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a campaign">
    Browse campaigns and pick one you want to support.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contribute">
    Enter the amount you want to back and confirm the transaction. Funds move from your wallet into the campaign’s escrow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track progress">
    Follow milestone updates. If the campaign uses community validation, you may be able to vote on milestone releases.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips

- Back only what you can afford to commit. Refunds depend on the campaign’s rules (e.g., goal not met, cancellation).
- Check the creator’s [reputation](/concepts/reputation-system) and campaign history when available.
