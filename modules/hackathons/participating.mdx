---
title: "Participating"
description: "How to participate in a hackathon on Boundless."
---

To participate in a Boundless hackathon, you register, build your project, and submit before the deadline. Prizes are awarded via the event’s [judging system](/modules/hackathons/judging-system); some events also have [post-event milestones](/modules/hackathons/post-event-milestones).

## Before you start

- [Set up your wallet](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet).
- Optional: [Form a hackathon team](/how-to-guides/form-a-hackathon-team).

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find an event">
    Browse open hackathons and check dates, tracks, and prize pools.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register">
    Register yourself or your team according to the event rules.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build">
    Build your project within the hackathon period. Follow the event’s theme and rules.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Submit your project (link, repo, demo, form) before the submission deadline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Judging and prizes">
    Judges score submissions; winners are announced. Prizes are released from escrow. If the event has [post-event milestones](/modules/hackathons/post-event-milestones), complete them to receive additional payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>
