Organizing a hackathon on Boundless involves creating the event, setting rules and prizes, configuring [judging](/modules/hackathons/judging-system), and optionally [post-event milestones](/modules/hackathons/post-event-milestones). Prize funds are locked in [trustless escrow](/concepts/trustless-escrow-explained).

## Before you start

- [Set up your wallet](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet) and ensure you have [Power Cells](/concepts/power-cells) and XLM for prizes.
- Define your event: theme, duration, tracks, and prize structure.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the event">
    Set the event name, dates, description, and rules. Define tracks or categories if applicable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set prizes">
    Define prize amounts and lock them in escrow. Specify how many winners and how prizes are split.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure judging">
    Set up the [judging system](/modules/hackathons/judging-system): criteria, judges, and scoring method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optional: post-event milestones">
    If you want follow-up work (e.g., mainnet deployment, docs), configure [post-event milestones](/modules/hackathons/post-event-milestones).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Open registration and announce the hackathon. Manage submissions and judging until the event ends.
  </Step>
</Steps>
