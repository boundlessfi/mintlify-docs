---
title: "Milestone delivery"
description: "How to deliver grant milestones and receive payouts on Boundless."
---

For milestone-based grants, you complete each milestone, submit evidence, and receive the corresponding payout when the milestone is approved.

## Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete the milestone">
    Do the work described in the milestone (e.g., deliverable, report, demo).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    Submit your deliverable through the grant page link, file, or form as required by the program.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    The program reviewer (or committee) reviews your submission against the milestone criteria.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payout">
    If approved, the milestone portion is released from escrow to your wallet. If not, you may get feedback and resubmit if the program allows.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips

- Match your submission to the milestone description to avoid delays or rejections.
- See [Structure grant milestones](/how-to-guides/structure-grant-milestones) for how to define clear milestones when applying.

## Disputes

If you disagree with a rejection, see [Handle disputes](/how-to-guides/handle-disputes).
