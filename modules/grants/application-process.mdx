Grant applications on Boundless are submitted to a [grant program](/modules/grants/grant-programs). Programs define eligibility, focus areas, and review criteria.

## Before you apply

- [Set up your wallet](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet).
- Read the program’s scope, [grant types](/modules/grants/grant-types), and requirements.
- Optional: [Build your reputation](/how-to-guides/build-your-reputation).

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a program">
    Browse [grant programs](/modules/grants/grant-programs) and pick one that matches your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare your application">
    Write your project description, goals, milestones (if applicable), timeline, and any requested attachments or links.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Submit the application before the deadline. You may need to connect your wallet or verify identity depending on the program.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and decision">
    Program reviewers evaluate applications. If accepted, you’ll get instructions for [milestone delivery](/modules/grants/milestone-delivery) and payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips

- Align your milestones with the program’s [grant type](/modules/grants/grant-types) and use [Structure grant milestones](/how-to-guides/structure-grant-milestones) for clarity.
- Answer all required fields and follow word limits or format rules.
