---
title: "FAQ: Crowdfunding"
description: "Frequently asked questions about crowdfunding on Boundless."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is crowdfunding on Boundless?">
    Creators run [campaigns](/modules/crowdfunding/overview) with a funding goal. Backers contribute XLM; funds are held in escrow and released according to the campaign’s [milestone structure](/modules/crowdfunding/milestone-structure) when the creator delivers and (if applicable) [community validation](/modules/crowdfunding/community-validation) passes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I back a campaign?">
    Choose a campaign, enter the amount you want to contribute, and confirm. See [Backing campaigns](/modules/crowdfunding/backing-campaigns).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When does the creator get the funds?">
    According to the campaign’s [milestone structure](/modules/crowdfunding/milestone-structure). The creator submits evidence for each milestone; when it’s approved (and any community validation is met), that portion is released from escrow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is community validation?">
    A process where the community (e.g., backers) can vote or signal on milestone releases or other campaign decisions. See [Community validation](/modules/crowdfunding/community-validation).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Refund rules depend on the campaign (e.g., goal not met, cancellation). Check the campaign details and platform policy. Disputes can be raised via [Handle disputes](/how-to-guides/handle-disputes).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
