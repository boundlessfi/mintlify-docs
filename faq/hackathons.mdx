---
title: "FAQ: Hackathons"
description: "Frequently asked questions about hackathons on Boundless."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a Boundless hackathon?">
    A time-boxed event where participants build projects and compete for prizes. Prizes are held in escrow and released to winners. Some events have [post-event milestones](/modules/hackathons/post-event-milestones) for extra payouts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I participate as a team?">
    Yes, when the event allows it. See [Form a hackathon team](/how-to-guides/form-a-hackathon-team) and [Participating](/modules/hackathons/participating).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How are winners chosen?">
    Organizers set up a [judging system](/modules/hackathons/judging-system) with criteria and judges. Submissions are scored and ranked; top projects win according to the prize structure.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What are post-event milestones?">
    Optional follow-up deliverables (e.g., mainnet deployment) that winners can complete after the hackathon to unlock additional payouts. See [Post-event milestones](/modules/hackathons/post-event-milestones).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I organize a hackathon?">
    See [Organizing](/modules/hackathons/organizing). You’ll create the event, set prizes in escrow, configure judging, and optionally add post-event milestones.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
