> **TL;DR:** Quick answers to what Boundless is, whether you need crypto experience, what XLM is, and how to convert earnings or check availability in your country.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Boundless?">
    Boundless is a platform on the Stellar network for bounties, grants, hackathons, and crowdfunding. It uses trustless escrow, reputation, and managed wallets (the platform holds your balance; passkey sign-in is planned for the future).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need crypto experience?">
    You don't need any. Get started with the [quick start](/getting-started/quick-start) and [set up your account](/how-to-guides/set-up-your-wallet). The app guides you through the rest Boundless manages your balance, so there's no wallet or seed phrase to set up.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is XLM?">
    XLM (Stellar Lumens) is the native asset of the Stellar network. On Boundless it’s used for rewards, payments, and fees. You earn XLM on the platform (it goes to your Boundless balance), or you can add it via the app when that's supported.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I convert earnings to fiat?">
    **Off-ramping** (XLM -> fiat) is an **upcoming feature**. When it's available, you'll need to [complete KYC](/how-to-guides/complete-kyc-verification) first (Boundless uses [Didit](https://didit.me/) for verification). **Withdrawing** your Stellar assets from the platform (e.g., to an external wallet) **already requires KYC**. See [Off-ramping](/concepts/off-ramping) and [Supported countries](/reference/supported-countries).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is Boundless available in my country?">
    Platform access and features (e.g., off-ramping) depend on your region. See [Supported countries](/reference/supported-countries) and check the app for your location.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
