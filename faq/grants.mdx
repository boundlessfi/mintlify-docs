---
title: "FAQ: Grants"
description: "Frequently asked questions about grants on Boundless."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a grant on Boundless?">
    A grant is application-based funding. You apply to a [grant program](/modules/grants/grant-programs); if accepted, you deliver [milestones](/modules/grants/milestone-delivery) and receive payouts from escrow as they’re approved.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What grant types exist?">
    Common types are milestone-based (payouts per milestone), one-time (single payout after delivery), and program-specific structures. See [Grant types](/modules/grants/grant-types).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get paid for a grant?">
    Complete each milestone and submit for review. When the milestone is approved, that portion of the grant is released from escrow to your wallet. See [Milestone delivery](/modules/grants/milestone-delivery).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My milestone was rejected. What can I do?">
    Check the feedback from the reviewer. You may be able to resubmit if the program allows. If you believe it was unfair, see [Handle disputes](/how-to-guides/handle-disputes).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I structure milestones?">
    See [Structure grant milestones](/how-to-guides/structure-grant-milestones) for tips on defining clear, verifiable milestones.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
